Trajectory Tracking

Key Achievements

  • Processed GPS trajectory data with Pandas and coordinate transformation algorithms
  • Implemented RDP algorithm for trajectory optimization and multi-track processing
  • Developed FastAPI service with Plotly visualizations for trajectory tracking dashboards
  • Performed A/B testing using sensor data to bring insights for tire wear and performance

Languages

  • A/B Testing
